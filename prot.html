COVID-19

PROTECTION

PROTECTION:

1.Keep cleaning your hands. Use soap and water or an alcohol solution to sterilize the hands.

2.Keep a safe distance from anyone coughing or sneezing.

3.Do not touch your eyes, nose, or mouth.

4.Make sure to cover your mouth and nose by bending the elbow or a tissue with coughing or sneezing.

5.Go home if you feel sick.

6.If you have a fever, cough, and trouble breathing, seek medical attention. And call the care facility before you go.

7. Follow the directions of the local health authorities.
