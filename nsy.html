COVID-19

NEW SYMPTOMS

The most helpful symptoms are:

1-Fever
2-Dry cough
3-Exhaustion

Less likely symptoms:

1-Pains and aches
2-Sore throat
3-Diarrhea
4-conjunctivitis
5- a headache
6-Losing a sense of taste or smell
7-Skin rash, or a change in the color of the fingers or toes

Serious symptoms:

1-Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
2-Chest pain or pressure
3-Loss of speech or movement
4- Nursing immediate medical care if you have severe symptoms. Always contact a physician or health facility before going to him.
5-People with mild symptoms should treat their symptoms at home even if they are in good health.
6-They receive an average of 5-6 days since symptoms appear, but it may take up to 14 days for symptoms to appear.
